# CampusRemind Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 7, 2026

## Overview

CampusRemind is designed with privacy as a core principle. Your data stays on your device.

## Data Collection

CampusRemind does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. There is no account creation, no analytics, no tracking, and no advertising.

## Information the App Uses

### iCal Calendar URL
You provide a calendar export URL from your institution's learning management system (e.g., Moodle). This URL is stored locally on your device in the app's configuration file. The app makes network requests only to this URL to fetch your calendar data.

### Apple Reminders
CampusRemind requests access to Apple Reminders to create and manage reminder lists for your course assignments. Reminder data is managed by the Apple Reminders system and subject to Apple's privacy policy.

### On-Device AI Summarization
If enabled, CampusRemind uses Apple's on-device Foundation Models to summarize assignment descriptions. All processing happens locally on your device — no data is sent to any external service for this feature.

## Network Requests

The app makes a single type of network request: fetching your iCal calendar feed from the URL you provide. This request goes directly to your institution's server. No data is sent to any third-party server, and no data is sent to the developer.

## Data Storage

All configuration data (your calendar URL, excluded courses, preferences) is stored locally on your device. No data is synced to any cloud service by the app.

## Data Deletion

- Use the **Reconfigure** button in Settings to delete your current configuration.
- Uninstalling the app removes all app data from your device.

## Third-Party Services

CampusRemind does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics frameworks.

## Children's Privacy

CampusRemind does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

Any updates to this privacy policy will be reflected in this document with an updated date.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the project's GitHub repository.
